Stuffed Bell Peppers

I am going to share with you one of my favorite recipes. These peppers can easily be made vegetarian or vegan really you can put anything you want inside your peppers so have fun and make something delicious!

Photo credit: therecipecritc

Ingredients

  • 6 Large Bell Peppers (any color!)
  • 1 cup of cooked rice (any rice!)
  • 1 cup cheese (any cheese!)
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 cup mushrooms
  • 1/2 chopped onion
  • 8 oz. tomato sauce
  • 6 oz. black olives
  • seasoning: slat, pepper, garlic ect.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Fully cook beef with onion. Season to preference.
  3. Slice the top 1/2 in from the top of the peppers and cut out the stems from the top.
  4. Coat 9X13 baking dish with cooking oil or line with tin foil.
  5. Arrange peppers in baking dish.
  6. Pour tomato sauce evenly into peppers.
  7. In a bowl mix cooked beef, cooked rice, cheese, mushrooms, olives, seasoning and remaining tomato sauce.
  8. Fill each pepper then top peppers with extra cheese.
  9. Bake for about 45 min- 1 hour.
  10. Enjoy!
